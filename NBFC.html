Last Minute Hotel Booking Portal

Project Brief

Non-Banking Financial Companies (NBFC) (“Customer”) is an establishment that provides financial services and banking facilities without meeting the legal definition of a Bank in India. They are covered under the Banking regulations laid down by the Reserve Bank of India and provide banking services like loans, credit facilities, TFCs, retirement planning, investing and stocking in the money market.

Problem Statement

The customer was using hybrid -On-premise and Cloud services to run their business applications. A hybrid approach was selected due to the Reserve Bank of India’s regulatory requirement..

Solution

The customer approached Smera when AWS India data centre was opened. We helped them n a comprehensive migration including items such as EC2, security groups, RDS etc. In doing this, we worked closely with third-party application vendors

Another requirement was of data security on the internet hence customer needed to implement SSL. We managed this for them and obtained approval for SSL certificate in AWS Certificate Manager, while liaising with their external DNS manager. In addition, we use security best practices and tools such as enabling CloudWatch, CloudTrail, Load Balancer access logs, and hardening Security Groups on AWS..

We also used Waratek Runtime Application Self-protection (RASP) to safeguard applications from exploits in the OWASP Top Ten, the SANS Top 25, and other common attack vectors.

Benefits
  • Reduced “total cost of ownership” (TCO) by moving on premise infrastructure to cloud.
  • Enhanced security for all business applications and customer data with SSL and RASP.
  • Easy and secure application development and integration with single cloud infrastructure.
